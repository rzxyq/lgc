Schedule


The schedule for the Fall 2015 round of Let's Get Coffee is as follows:

Survey
New students and upperclassmen complete their respective surveys between September 6 and 12.
Select
Provided the website link by email, upperclassmen select their new students with similar interests.
Meet
Upperclassmen meet with their new student for at least 30 minutes at a public location on campus.
Reflect
Participating students are asked to complete a quick post-connection reflection survey.
